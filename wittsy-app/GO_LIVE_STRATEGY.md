1. **Risk: Low Retention**
   - **Mitigation:** Daily rewards, social features, push notifications
   - **Backup:** Aggressive content updates, events

   - **Backup:** Add subscription model, reduce prices

   - **Mitigation:** Focus on organic growth, referrals

   - **Mitigation:** Follow guidelines strictly
   - **Backup:** Have alternative versions ready

   - **Mitigation:** Excellent onboarding, responsive support
   - **Backup:** Address issues quickly, incentivize positive reviews
