# Audio Assets

This directory contains all audio files for the Wittsy app.

## Required Files

### Background Music
- `background-music.mp3` - Looping background music for the app (upbeat, gaming-style)

### Sound Effects
- `click.mp3` - Button click sound
- `success.mp3` - Success/achievement sound
- `error.mp3` - Error/failure sound
- `notification.mp3` - Notification alert
- `vote.mp3` - Vote submission sound
- `win.mp3` - Round/game win celebration
- `star.mp3` - Star achievement sound

## Audio Specifications

- **Format**: MP3 (recommended for compatibility)
- **Sample Rate**: 44.1kHz
- **Bit Rate**: 128-192 kbps
- **Duration**: 
  - Background music: 2-3 minutes (will loop)
  - Sound effects: 0.5-2 seconds

## Where to Get Audio

### Free Resources
- **Freesound.org** - Community sound library
- **Zapsplat.com** - Free sound effects
- **Incompetech.com** - Royalty-free music by Kevin MacLeod
- **Purple Planet Music** - Free music for content creators
- **Bensound.com** - Royalty-free music

### Recommended Style
- **Background Music**: Upbeat, energetic, gaming/competitive vibe (similar to League of Legends, Wild Rift)
- **Sound Effects**: Clean, modern UI sounds with a slight futuristic/tech feel

## Placeholder Files

Until you add real audio files, the app will gracefully handle missing files by:
1. Logging warnings to console
2. Continuing to function without audio
3. Still showing audio controls in settings

## Adding Files

1. Download or create your audio files
2. Name them exactly as listed above
3. Place them in this directory
4. Restart the app to load the new audio
