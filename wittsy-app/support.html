How do I play Wittz?

Wittz is a multiplayer word game where you complete funny phrases and vote for the best answers. Join a game room with 3-8 players, submit your creative responses, and vote for your favorites!

How do I join a game?

  • Tap "Quick Play" for instant matchmaking
  • Browse available rooms on the home screen
  • Create your own custom room
  • Join ranked matches to compete on the leaderboard

What are coins used for?

Coins can be used to unlock avatar items, purchase cosmetics, and customize your profile. Earn coins by:

  • Winning games
  • Daily login rewards
  • Completing achievements
  • Climbing the leaderboard

How does ranked mode work?

Ranked matches use an ELO rating system. Win games to increase your rating and climb the seasonal leaderboard. Your rank is based on your performance against other players.

Can I play with friends?

Yes! Create a custom room and share the room code with your friends, or invite them directly through the app.

What if I encounter a bug?

Please email us at support@wittz.com with:

  • Description of the issue
  • Device type and OS version
  • Screenshots if possible
