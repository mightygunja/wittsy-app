Frequently Asked Questions

What is Wittz?

Wittz is a real-time multiplayer word game where players compete by crafting the wittiest phrases to match creative prompts. Battle friends, climb the leaderboard, and prove you have the sharpest wit!

How do I create an account?

You can sign up using your email address, Google account, or Apple ID. You can also play as a guest without creating an account.

How do I play?

  1. Join or create a game room
  2. Wait for the prompt to appear
  3. Submit your wittiest phrase before time runs out
  4. Vote on other players' submissions
  5. The player with the most votes wins the round!

What are coins used for?

Coins are the in-game currency used to unlock avatar items, accessories, and other cosmetic upgrades in the Avatar Shop. You earn coins by playing games, completing daily rewards, and achieving milestones.

How does the ranking system work?

Wittz uses an ELO-based rating system. Win games to increase your rating and climb through the ranks: Bronze, Silver, Gold, Platinum, Diamond, and Legend.

Can I play with friends?

Yes! You can add friends, create private rooms, and invite them to play. You can also join public rooms to compete against other players.

How do I report inappropriate content?

If you encounter inappropriate content or behavior, please email us at support@wittz.app with details of the incident. We take content moderation seriously and will investigate all reports.
