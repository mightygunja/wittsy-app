User Data Deletion Instructions

Your Right to Data Deletion

At Wittz, we respect your privacy and your right to control your personal data. You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time.

What Data Will Be Deleted?

When you request account deletion, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Your profile information (username, email, avatar)
  • Game history and statistics
  • Achievements and progress
  • Friend lists and social connections
  • In-app purchase records (where applicable)
  • All user-generated content (phrases, votes, etc.)

How to Delete Your Account

Method 1: In-App Deletion (Recommended)

  1. Open the Wittz app
  2. Go to Settings (gear icon)
  3. Scroll to Account section
  4. Tap "Delete Account"
  5. Confirm your decision
  6. Your account will be deleted immediately

Method 2: Email Request

If you cannot access the app, send an email to:

Email: support@wittz.app

Subject: Account Deletion Request

Include:

  • Your registered email address
  • Your username (if you remember it)
  • Reason for deletion (optional)

We will process your request within 30 days and send you a confirmation email.

Important Notes

  • Permanent Action: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone
  • Data Retention: Some data may be retained for legal or security purposes (e.g., transaction records for tax compliance)
  • Third-Party Services: If you signed in with Google, Apple, or Facebook, you may need to revoke app access from those platforms separately
  • Active Subscriptions: Cancel any active subscriptions before deleting your account to avoid future charges

Data Retention Period

After account deletion:

  • Immediate: Your profile becomes inaccessible
  • 30 days: All personal data is permanently deleted from our servers
  • 90 days: Backup copies are purged from our backup systems

Some anonymized analytics data may be retained indefinitely for service improvement purposes, but this data cannot be linked back to you.
